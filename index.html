About Citanova

Company Overview

Citanova is a premium independent financial information consultancy firm registered and operated from Liverpool, United Kingdom. Founded in 2015 to bridge the gap between fragmented market data and actionable investment strategy, we specialise in transparent, unbiased financial intelligence for all client tiers.


We adhere to strict UK financial ethics standards, prioritising data accuracy, client confidentiality and long-term partnership over short-term transaction gains. Our multi-disciplinary team combines quantitative analysts, compliance specialists and macroeconomic researchers to deliver comprehensive advisory output.


Since inception, we have grown from a 3-person boutique firm to a team of 28 financial professionals, managing over £450M in client assets across our advisory portfolios and maintaining a 98% client retention rate – a testament to our commitment to results and relationship-building.

Our Financial Consultancy Services

1. Institutional Market Intelligence Reports

Custom weekly, monthly and quarterly deep-dive analysis covering global equity markets, macroeconomic trends, interest rate forecasts and geopolitical financial risk mapping.

Each report includes: 5+ proprietary market indicators, comparative analysis against 5-year historical trends, scenario modelling for 3 macroeconomic outcomes, and actionable recommendations tailored to your institutional risk appetite.

Deliverables: Interactive dashboards, PDF briefings, monthly live analyst briefings (in-person or virtual), and ad-hoc alerts for market-moving events.

2. Private Client Wealth Advisory

Personalised asset allocation reviews, portfolio risk auditing, cross-border tax information and long-term wealth preservation planning for high-net-worth individuals.

Our private client service includes a dedicated wealth advisor, quarterly portfolio reviews, tax optimisation strategies aligned with UK and international tax law, and estate planning integration to ensure intergenerational wealth transfer efficiency.

Minimum asset threshold: £500,000 in investable assets (waived for family office clients).

3. SME Business Financial Consultancy

Cash flow forecasting, market expansion feasibility studies, capital raising intelligence and industry benchmark financial data for Liverpool and UK small-medium enterprises.

We specialise in supporting SMEs in manufacturing, tech, and retail sectors, with services including: 12/24/36-month cash flow modelling, grant/funding identification, competitor financial benchmarking, and pricing strategy optimisation.

Success rate: 89% of SME clients report improved cash flow within 6 months of engagement.

4. Regulatory & Compliance Information Support

Updated guidance on UK financial regulations, cross-border investment compliance, reporting requirements and risk control framework design.

Our compliance team tracks all FCA, PRA and EU regulatory changes, providing proactive updates and implementation support to ensure your business remains compliant. Services include compliance audit preparation, policy drafting, and staff training modules.

We also offer Brexit-specific compliance support for UK-EU cross-border transactions.

5. Real-Time Custom Market Data Feeds

On-demand filtered market data dashboards, live price tracking, volatility alerts and custom statistical modelling for active traders and investment funds.

Customisation options: Asset class filtering (equities, FX, commodities, crypto), alert thresholds (price, volume, volatility), data visualisation preferences, and integration with third-party trading platforms (Bloomberg, Reuters, MetaTrader).

Uptime guarantee: 99.98% service availability with 24/7 technical support for enterprise clients.

Retainer-Based Model

Project-Based Model
